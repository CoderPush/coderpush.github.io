We’re looking for a Product Designer with 2+ years of experience to join with our software teams to build exciting products in eCommerce, social network and MarketPlace platforms.

We are a remote-first company! It means you are encouraged to work from anywhere that you enjoy the most. Whenever you feel like you need some human interaction, our crew in Vietnam and the US will be happy seeing you!

Who we are#

CoderPush is a studio of engineers and designers who collaborate with clients for great software, teams, and processes.

We set a very high expectation on client satisfaction for the long term. See reviews on Clutch.

What you’ll work on#

Within the last 12 months, we have helped launch 13 products. Here’s some of them:

  • Sift Alerts - curated news tracking for companies you care about
  • Joe Coffee - helps you discover local indie coffee shops
  • Maple Healthcare - helps you keep a healthy neck posture while using your phone!

They are not perfect, and that’s exactly why we need you! Come join us to make better products together!

We are looking for someone with#

  • 2 years of experience drawing, deleting, re-drawing again and helped push software products to users.
  • Ability to draw wireframes, sketches and UI design.
  • Good communication skills in English and Vietnamese.
  • Humility to learn and share what you don’t know, everyday.
  • Ability to understand and define all actions to be performed when assigned to a task.
  • Ability to manage time when facing difficulties, under pressure or deadlines.
  • Ability to communicate with Stakeholders and users to gather requirements.
  • Ability to creatively creating solutions for problem presented to.
  • Curiosity and patience when working with Stakeholders and our beloved Developers.

That’s a long list, and we don’t expect you to check all the boxes, we are here to learn with you!

Benefits#

  • Salary $1.5k-$2k gross depending on experience.
  • Performance-based bonuses tied to project deliverables.
  • Sponsorships to get learning resources.
  • Full-time insurances and benefits.
  • Sponsorships to take part in sports and tech activities.

Office#

We are remote-first, it means working remotely does not make you less important or boring. But people who love office still get a great office experience. Our humble place is in the beautiful City Garden in Binh Thanh district. We hold weekly video and board games every Friday from 4pm till late – a great time to host friends, clients and job applicants!

Personal growth#

Still here? You must be thinking about how you’ll grow. That’s one of our core values.

Why join a young company instead of a big one? Because you can learn faster, make mistakes, share learning from mistakes, and have personal impact on those around you. You shape how the company forms.

Why a software studio instead of a product company? You do not get stuck in a product or a product team. You get coaching and mentorship not from only your project team but from other project teams as well. We build to last, without the pressure of investment capital.

Why CoderPush instead of just another “devshop”? Because we build teams first, products second. We have been cofounders of a bilingual school, a coding school, a running club, and a few funded startups (let’s exchange stories!).

And lastly, join to build a company like building great code itself: continuous improvement, continuous deployment, refactoring, technical debt, and contributing to open source.
